Because my website was for a location based business I was able to use reviews to gain Googles trust. Google takes reviews from local listings into serious consideration in order to give users the best results. My competitors were much more established than me so they already had lots of reviews from years prior. However, they were not intentionally trying to gain more reviews, at least not effectively. Because I saw this as a strong signal to google I made it a point for employees interacting with customers to mention reviews. Never incentives or offer anything in return, but just mention that if they gave feedback online it would help our business.
